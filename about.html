Who we are

We are a small textile printing company who is providing custom graphic design, screen printing, embroidery, and digital printing services to all that are needing custom designs.

What we do

We offer a variety of services. If you do not have a logo we can create one. Need shirts printed for your next event? We will be able to screen print that for you in no time as well as keeping the quality high. Embroidery is always a great way to keep your brand looking sleek and professional. Or are you just looking for more promotional merchandising, we offer a variety of digital printing options for just that!

Our History

Our history started off with just a computer, printer, and home made screen printing equipment. We have slowly built up our brand through the years of providing a quality service at a friendly price for any new business or artist looking to further their brand.Through the years we have grown our own brand to a much larger scale of operation to be able to keep up with the demand of new projects of returning customers, as well as new customers looking for something different.

What we look to achieve

With our brand growing rapidly and expanding in to new territories, we are looking to provide an easy website for customers to place their order in an easier way then having to drive to our warehouse. This site also gives us the opportunity to provide quality custom products on a much larger scale. All of North America is welcome to check us out and give us a try.
